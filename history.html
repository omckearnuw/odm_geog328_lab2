History

Prior to European colonization, the area was inhabited by the Piscataway people. In 1751, the first settlement in the area, Georgetown, was settled. Over time, the area grew in population. In 1790, the area was designated the capital district. In 1791, the city began to be designed by Pierre Charles L'Enfant.
